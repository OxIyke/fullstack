Top 3 Movies of All Time

Note: i only wach anime

Black Clover

Black Clover is an anime series that follows the story of Asta, a young boy born without magic in a world where magic is everything. Despite this, Asta dreams of becoming the Wizard King, the strongest mage in the Clover Kingdom. The series is filled with action, adventure, and themes of friendship and perseverance.

Tonikaku Kawaii

Tonikaku Kawaii, also known as "Fly Me to the Moon," is a romantic comedy anime that tells the story of Nasa Yuzaki and Tsukasa Tsukuyomi. After a chance encounter, Nasa proposes to Tsukasa, and they get married shortly after. The series explores their married life filled with humor, love, and heartwarming moments.
